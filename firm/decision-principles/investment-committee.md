# Investment Committee Principles

This file should define how the firm wants decisions captured.

## Suggested Decision Standard

Every investment or pass should leave behind:

1. The core belief
2. The key disconfirming risks
3. What evidence would change the view
4. Who owns the next action
5. When the company should be revisited

## Why This Matters

For agents, the quality of future retrieval depends on explicit decision history. A strong `decision-log.md` is often more valuable than a long memo without a clear conclusion.
