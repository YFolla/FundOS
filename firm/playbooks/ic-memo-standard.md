# IC Memo Standard

Use this as a reference for how company memos should be structured.

## Recommended Sections

1. Company snapshot
2. Founder and team
3. Market and timing
4. Product and wedge
5. Commercial traction
6. Why now for the firm
7. Risks and open questions
8. Proposed decision
9. Next step

## Agent Rule

When drafting a memo, start from the company index entry and only load the linked files that support these sections.
