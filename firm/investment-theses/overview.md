# Investment Thesis Overview

This folder should hold durable firm-level theses that are broader than one company.

## Suggested Structure

- one file per enduring thesis
- one sentence on why the thesis matters now
- explicit risks and disconfirming evidence
- links to the market-theme folders that operationalize the thesis

## Seed Thesis Areas

- AI application layer
- embedded fintech
- digital asset infrastructure
- software for regulated or operationally complex industries
