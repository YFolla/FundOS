# Operating Cadence

This file should describe the recurring outputs that keep the TeamOS current.

## Suggested Cadence

- Daily: log founder interactions and important follow-ups for active companies
- Weekly: update company next steps, diligence status, and new market learnings
- Biweekly: refresh portfolio support plans and decision logs
- Monthly: update market theses, valuation notes, and fund-reporting artifacts
- Quarterly: produce LP updates and clean stale records

## Hard Gates

- A new company is not "in process" until it has an index entry and canonical folder.
- An IC discussion is not complete until the decision rationale is captured in `decision-log.md`.
- A portfolio support request is not complete until the portfolio plan reflects the latest action owner and date.
